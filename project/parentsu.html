Education Rights

The Right to Education (RTE) Act is a landmark legislation in India that ensures free and compulsory education for all children aged 6 to 14 years. It guarantees every child the right to quality education, regardless of their background, and holds schools accountable for providing a safe and inclusive learning environment.
