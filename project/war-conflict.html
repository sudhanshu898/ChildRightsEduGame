War & Conflict Rights

Learn About War & Conflict Rights

Children have special rights during times of war and conflict. These include:

  • The right to protection from violence and harm
  • The right to humanitarian assistance
  • The right to education even during conflict
  • The right to family reunification
  • The right to psychological support and recovery

International laws and organizations work to protect children in conflict zones by:

  • Preventing recruitment of child soldiers
  • Ensuring access to food, water, and medical care
  • Providing safe spaces for learning and play
  • Supporting mental health and trauma recovery
