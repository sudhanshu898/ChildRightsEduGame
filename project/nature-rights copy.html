Nature Rights

Learn About Nature Rights

Nature rights are important because:

  • Every living thing has a right to exist
  • Ecosystems need protection
  • Clean air and water are essential
  • Biodiversity keeps nature healthy
  • Future generations need a healthy planet

Ways to protect nature:

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • Save water and energy
  • Protect wildlife habitats
  • Plant trees and gardens
  • Learn about local ecosystems
