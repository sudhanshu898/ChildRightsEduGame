Neuro Rights

Learn About Neuro Rights

Neuro rights protect our minds and thoughts:

  • Right to mental privacy
  • Protection of brain data
  • Freedom of thought
  • Right to mental identity
  • Equal access to brain health care

Why neuro rights matter:

  • Protecting our thoughts and memories
  • Ensuring safe brain technology use
  • Supporting mental well-being
  • Preventing misuse of brain data
  • Promoting fair treatment
