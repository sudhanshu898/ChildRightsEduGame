Learn About Technology and AI Rights

Technology and AI rights are important for children because:

  • Right to privacy and data protection
  • Safe access to digital resources
  • Protection from online harm
  • Digital literacy education
  • Fair and ethical AI systems

Ways to stay safe with technology:

  • Using strong passwords
  • Being careful with personal information
  • Understanding digital footprints
  • Recognizing trusted sources
  • Balancing screen time
