Understanding Climate Rights

Climate rights are about ensuring everyone, especially children, has access to:

  • Clean air and water
  • A healthy environment
  • Protection from climate disasters
  • Education about environmental issues
  • A voice in climate decisions

Children can help protect climate rights by:

  • Learning about climate change
  • Reducing waste and recycling
  • Saving energy and water
  • Participating in environmental activities
  • Sharing knowledge with others
