Genetic Rights

Learn About Genetic Rights

Genetic rights are important because they protect:

  • Privacy of genetic information
  • Right to know about genetic conditions
  • Protection from genetic discrimination
  • Access to genetic testing when needed
  • Ethical use of genetic research

Understanding genetics helps us:

  • Know about inherited traits
  • Understand family health history
  • Make informed health decisions
  • Respect genetic diversity
  • Support medical research
