Family Rights

Learn About Family Rights

Family rights ensure that children have a safe and nurturing family environment. These rights include:

  • Right to parental care and guidance
  • Right to maintain family relationships
  • Right to family support and protection
  • Right to family privacy
  • Right to cultural and family identity

Understanding family rights helps ensure children grow up in loving, supportive environments that promote their wellbeing and development.
