Learn About Space Rights

Space rights are about:

  • Fair access to space exploration
  • Protecting space environments
  • Sharing space discoveries
  • Space safety and security
  • International cooperation in space

Why space rights matter:

  • Future of space exploration
  • Scientific discoveries
  • Space resources management
  • Peaceful use of space
  • Benefits for all humanity
